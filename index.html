Kaaf Vision

To be the leading company in empowering recent graduates through distinguished training programs that enhance their technical and personal skills and help them integrate smoothly into the job market

Our Mission

To provide recent graduates with the technical and personal skills necessary to succeed in the job market, through high-quality training programs specifically designed to meet their needs.

Goals

Empowering Graduates, Building Ismailia's Future

To develop the technical skills of recent graduates, such as programming, data analysis, and others.

To improve the personal skills of recent graduates, such as communication, teamwork, and critical thinking.

To help recent graduates gain practical experience through training and mentoring programs.

Timeline Achievement

Kaaf Timeline

Kaaf is a new initiative from ElHaiez Makerspace, a company dedicated to empowering and building the capacities of Ismailia community since 2018

  • 2023

    2023

    Breaking New Ground

    Developed the humanoid robot ADAM.

    Entrepreneurial Spirit

    Launched the TECH TiTANS competition in association with ITI, focusing on entrepreneurship and technology (60+ participants).

    Sharpening Skills

    Continued the "تكلم حتى أراك" and Robomania competitions, with "تكلم حتى أراك" exceeding 90 participants.

    Growing Impact

    Trained over 1200 participants and provided online programs to over 80 Arab youth participants in the USA

  • 2022

    2022

    Media Presence

    Built robot TOOT, the first robot TV presenter in the MENA region, in association with EBBAMS for DMC channel.

    Competition Success

    Continued the GATE RCS Ismailia, "تكلم حتى أراك", and Robomania competitions, with participation exceeding 90 for both GATE RCS and "تكلم حتى أراك" and reaching over 1000 for Robomania.

    Reaching New Audiences

    Delivered training programs to over 1000 participants and offered online programs to over 50 Arab youth participants in the USA.

  • 2021

    2021

    Local Expertise, Global Impact

    Continued co-hosting NASA Space Apps Ismailia.

    Fostering Innovation

    Established the GATE RCS Ismailia competition (underwater ROVs) with over 75 participants.

    Communication is Key

    Launched the " تكلم حتى أراك" (Speak So I Can See You) competition to develop presentation and soft skills (40+ participants).

    Expanding Robotics

    Organized the Robomania competition.

    Investing in the Future

    Trained over 900 participants.

  • 2020

    2020

    Innovation in Action

    Manufactured a robot to assist patients during the COVID-19 pandemic at Suez Canal University Hospital.

    Life-Saving Technology

    Developed a ventilator device for patients at Suez Canal University Hospital.

    Global Recognition

    Reached the International Semi-Finals in Technovation Girls (Technology and Entrepreneurship for Girls).

    Adapting to Challenges

    Shifted to online training programs, reaching over 650 participants.

  • 2019

    2019

    Expanding Horizons

    Participated in the ROV MATE competition with the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (Alexandria) and the Minesweepers competition.

    Continued Growth

    Co-hosted NASA Space Apps Ismailia again and created training programs for over 600 participants.

  • 2018

    2018

    Hitting the Ground Running

    Co-hosted NASA Space Apps Ismailia and mentored Team 57357 Rangers in the ROV MATE competition.

    Building Skills

    Achieved first place in the Robomania competition, showcasing the talent within the Ismailia community.
